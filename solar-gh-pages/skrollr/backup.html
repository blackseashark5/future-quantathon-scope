SPACE BASED SOLAR POWER

Solar power directly from space may arrive sooner than you think.

Did You Know?

Every hour, more solar energy reaches the earth than the earth uses in a year.

About 30%

of this energy is reflected back into space by the atmosphere.

Waste Not

Since clouds, atmosphere and nighttime are absent in space, satellite-based solar panels would be able to capture and the store substantially more energy than terrestrial solar panels.

How Does it Work?

Launch solar panels into orbit aboard energy transmitting satellites. Collect high intensity, uninterrupted solar radiation. Giant mirrors reflect huge amounts of solar radiation onto smaller solar collectors. This radiation is then wirelessly beamed to earth in a safe and controlled was as either a microwave or laser beam.
